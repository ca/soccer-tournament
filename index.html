Madison Holleran Memorial Soccer Tournament

the Madison Holleran Memorial Tournament is

a soccer tournament held on the Northern Highlands Regional High School turf field. It is a 5v5 soccer tournament using pug goals and small-sided fields. Everyone who participates will receive a t-shirt, and the winning team will receive a gift basket of gift cards to various stores/restaurants.

Our purpose

To raise money for the Madison Holleran foundation, while remembering Madison and the sport she loved.

And to have a really good time playing soccer with our friends!

Northern Highlands Turf Field

Rules

  1. No fouling of any kind.

    Slide tackling or severe fouls will result in immediate removal from the tournament.

  2. Absolutely no alcohol or illegal substances

    will be tolerated during the tournament. The user or holder will be immediately removed from the tournament.

  3. 15 minute games

    to be played with no half time.

  4. Typical soccer regulations

    and no arguing with the referee will be tolerated.

  5. If a waiver is not signed

    and submitted prior to the date of the tournament, the player who failed to do so will not be allowed to participate.

  6. On a lighter note, have fun!
